What Is NIKE Company?

It is a global company founded in 1964 in the name of blue ribbon sports It was modified and renamed in 1971 under its current name NIKE It is a joint stock company headquartered in the United States of America and has won many awards, the most important of which is national design awards

There are its subsidiaries Converse, Cole Haan, Umbro and Air Jordan, and its products are footwear, sports equipment and clothing, and it has branches all over the world.
